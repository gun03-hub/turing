About HuntX

HuntX is an exciting campus treasure hunt where teams of two compete against other teams to solve riddles and clues hidden across college landmarks. The event tests teamwork, logical thinking, observation, and strategy. The first team to solve all clues and reach the final destination wins the hunt.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team must consist of 2 players.
  • Only 4–5 teams can participate in one round.
  • Teams must stay together at all times.
  • No use of mobile phones or external help is allowed.
  • Clues must not be removed or damaged.
  • Fair play is mandatory.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of unfair means or external assistance.
  • Tampering with clues or misleading other teams.
  • Team splitting up during the hunt.

Format & Winning Criteria

Format

  • The event begins with a rule explanation.
  • Each team receives the first clue at the starting point.
  • Solving each clue leads to the next location.
  • Teams must follow the sequence until the final destination.
  • The team completing all clues first wins.

Device & Connectivity

  • Mobile phones are not allowed during the hunt.
  • No internet or communication devices permitted.
  • Teams rely only on observation and problem-solving.

Scoring System

  • 1st team to finish – Winner.
  • 2nd team to finish – Runner-up.
  • If teams finish close together, clue accuracy will be considered.
  • Rule violations may lead to penalties or disqualification.

Winning Criteria

  • The first team to solve all clues and reach the final destination wins.
  • The second team reaching the destination becomes runner-up.
