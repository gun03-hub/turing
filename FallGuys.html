About Fall Guys Tournament

We are organizing a Fall Guys Tournament in our college with around 50 players for a fun and competitive gaming event. Players will compete in multiple rounds including obstacle courses, survival rounds, and elimination stages in custom lobbies. Points will be awarded based on performance in each round. The top players will advance to the finals where one ultimate winner will be crowned.

Rules & Guidelines

  • The tournament will be played using laptops only.
  • This tournament will be conducted strictly in Solo Mode.
  • The tournament is open to all registered college students.
  • Approximately 50 players will participate.
  • Participants must complete registration before the event starts.
  • Players must join the lobby on time.
  • No match restarts will be provided due to personal connectivity issues.
  • Players are responsible for their own internet connection.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Refusal to follow tournament schedule or organizer instructions.
  • Intentional teaming in Solo Mode.
  • Using hacks, cheats, scripts, or third-party software.
  • Modifying game files or manipulating gameplay.

Format & Winning Criteria

Format

  • The tournament will consist of 5 matches.
  • Players compete in obstacle and survival rounds.
  • Top players advance to the final round.

Device & Connectivity

  • Players must participate using laptops only.
  • A stable internet connection is required.
  • Connection issues during the match are the player's responsibility.

Scoring System

  • Winner of the final round receives 10 points.
  • All other finalists receive 5 points each.
  • Players eliminated before the final receive no points.

Winning Criteria

  • Total points will be calculated after all matches.
  • The player with the highest total points will be declared the winner.
  • Tie-breaker rules will be applied if scores are equal.
