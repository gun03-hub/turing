About Survival Supremacy - MAX Arena

Free Fire MAX is a fast-paced battle royale game where up to 50 players fight for survival on a remote island. Players can enter the battlefield as part of a 4-member squad, parachuting onto the map, looting weapons and equipment, and engaging in intense real-time combat. As the safe zone continuously shrinks, players are forced into closer encounters until only one squad remains victorious.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants must play with a full squad. Duo and Trio are not allowed.

Disqualification Criteria

  • If any team is found teaming up with another squad, they will be disqualified for that match.

Format & Winning Criteria

Format

  • 1 Match (BR-Rank) will be played in a custom room.

  • Do not rely on college WiFi for gameplay.

Scoring System

  • The winning squad will secure the first position.

Winning Criteria

  • The squad surviving till the end and securing the first position will be declared the winner.
  • The squad finishing second will be declared runner-up.
