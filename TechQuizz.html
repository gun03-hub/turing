About TECH QUIZZ

A fast-paced quiz competition designed to test participants’ knowledge of technology, gadgets, programming, and recent tech trends. Teams compete in three rounds that challenge their speed, accuracy, and technical awareness.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants must compete fairly without using internet or any external help.
  • Once an answer is given, it cannot be changed.
  • Teams must maintain discipline during the event.
  • Participants are advised to arrive 10 minutes before the event starts.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of internet or cheating during the quiz.
  • Misbehavior or disruption during the event.
  • Ignoring instructions of the organizers.

Format & Winning Criteria

Format

  • The quiz will consist of three rounds.
  • General Tech Round – Basic technology questions.
  • Logo / Guess the Tech Round – Identify tech companies, gadgets or software.
  • Rapid Fire Round – Quick questions in limited time.

Device & Connectivity

  • A website will be used for the buzzer system.
  • Only one mobile or laptop per team is required.

Scoring System

  • Correct answer = +10 points
  • Wrong answer = -1 point

Winning Criteria

  • The team with the highest total score after all rounds will be declared the winner.
